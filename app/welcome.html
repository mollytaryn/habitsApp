Welcome

This habit-tracking app is designed to help you lessen bad habits and establish good ones by giving you real data about your behaviors. Follow the instructions below to get started:

  1. Set up the good and bad habits you want to track. A habit can be anything from exercising for 30 minutes, to checking Facebook, to smoking. You name it.
  2. Every time you engage in one of your habits, tap the “+” button. This will count instances by day.
  3. Skip a good habit / resist a bad habit for a day? Before bed, click the “0” button to log a zero for the day. (Remember, when it comes to changing habits, not doing is just as important as doing!)
  4. Use the graph button to check on your progress. This data will help you take control of your habits so you can make real and lasting changes.
